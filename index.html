About Apartment Veve

Experience comfort and luxury in Privlaka

Apartment Veve is located in Privlaka, just 80 km from Kornati Marina and 81 km from Biograd Heritage Museum. Both free WiFi and on-site parking are available at the apartment free of charge.

The apartment features outdoor seating, a patio with garden views, air conditioning, and a balcony with sea views.
